Welcome to the Old Chou’s Treasure solver. Basically, it is modified version of Minesweeper. The only real difference is each spot can only have 1 or 2 bombs nearby and it only looks at spots in the “up”, “right”, “down” and "left" position. So, in game dig out one of the corners and see what happens and make this map match. So, if you get Cabbage, click that spot until it’s cabbage. The goal is to find the Treasure, which is hidden between 4 bombs.
